Hello, I am martin and this is a short information page about myself. I am currently preparing my masters thesis in architecture at the university of technology in graz, austria. I have been working as student assistent at the institute of architecture and media at TU-Graz. I’m lucky to have been brought up bilingually which made it easier for me to learn additional languages. my passion is all about 3d-design, parametric design, programming and evolutionary design on the software side and digital fabrication on the hardware side. i started out as a mechanical engineer student and have worked alongside my studies as engineering designer which eventually led me to the path i took by changing my studies to architecture. this allowed me to shift my main main focus to computational design and aesthetics. i am currently beginning my masters thesis. should you like more information about myself or my projects, or in general, feel free to send me an email, i’d be more than happy to help.
