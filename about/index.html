Hello, I am martin and I am currently preparing my master’s thesis in architecture at the university of technology in Graz, Austria. I have been working as a student assistant at the institute of architecture and media at TU Graz. My passion is all about 3d design, parametric design, programming and evolutionary design on the software side and digital fabrication on the hardware side. I started out as a mechanical engineer student and have worked alongside my studies as engineering designer which eventually led me to the path I took by switching studies to architecture. This allowed me to shift my focus to computational design and aesthetics. Should you like more information about me, my projects or general information, feel free to send me an email, I’d be more than happy to help.
