drone_mapping

08/2018

I have been flying drones and helicopters for many years, racing FPV drones and drone mapping of terrain and buildings for 3d reconstruction. The mapping is based on photogrammetry and not lidar based. I am used to work on big point clouds >5gb. My racing drones are built by myself although for reconstruction purposes commercial drones are used.
