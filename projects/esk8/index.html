eSk8

01/2021

topology optimization

this is a frame design for an electric skateboard that has been topology optimized to reduce weight and improve stiffness while offering enough space internally to house the electrical components and battery. the plugin used was t0pos. inputs to the model are applied forces, bearings and raw build volume. the system is cfd analised and material removed where the stress is least. results vary depending on resolution of voxels.
