prefab_concrete

02/2019

The system rombo is a modular structure consisting of 3D-printed concrete shells that are easy to stack and transport. These elements are placed on site, reinforced and then cast with in-situ concrete to form a solid beam grid.

the elements are fully configurable via grasshopper as well as the grid layout and shape of the quadripartite columns.
