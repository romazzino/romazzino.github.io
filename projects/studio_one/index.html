prefab_concrete

02/2019

The rombo system is a modular structure consisting of 3D-printed concrete shells that are easy to stack and transport. These elements are placed on site, steel reinforced and cast with in-situ concrete to form a solid beam grid. the advantages of this concept are quick building time, serialization and easy manufacturing of the elements in a factory as well as adaptability to size and shape of the project. The elements and the grid layout as well as the shape of the quadripartite columns are fully scripted.
